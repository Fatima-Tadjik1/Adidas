about us

ADIDAS is holding company.
Reebok Sportswear company, And the Taylormade Golf Equipment company and Rockport company are its subsidiaries.
ADIDAS is the largest manufacturer of sports equipment in Europe and the secoud largest manufacturer of sports equipment in the world after Nike.

ADIDAS was founded in 1948 by ADOLF DASSLER. With the help of his brother RUDOLPH, he started the production of sports shoes in 1924.
